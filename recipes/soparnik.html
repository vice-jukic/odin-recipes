Soparnik

Home

Description

This thin pie filled with chard and onion is a true tradicional treasure. Although modest in its ingredients, this dish is rich in flavor.

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 380 g of plain flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 240 ml of water

For the filling

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Mix water, oil, and salt until you get a smooth dough. Divide it into two parts and let it rest covered for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Wash the chard well, cut off the tough parts of the stems, and dry it. Slice it into strips and place it in a bowl. Add salt, mix, and let it sit for about 10 minutes, then drain the liquid
  3. "Add oil, chopped spring onions, parsley, black pepper to the chard, and mix well.
  4. On a floured surface, roll out half of the dough (round or square shape). Place it on a baking sheet (about Ø 38 cm). Spread the Swiss chard filling over the dough and cover it with the second rolled-out dough.
  5. Seal the edges of the dough and fold them under, then prick the surface of the dough with a fork. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for about 40 minutes.
  6. Brush the baked soparnik well with olive oil and sprinkle with chopped garlic.
