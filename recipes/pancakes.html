Description

This is a standard pancake recipe, where the key is to whisk the batter until smooth and cook them in a well-heated pan. You can use a cup instead of a kitchen scale for measuring ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 200 g of plain flour
  • 200 ml of warm milk
  • 200 ml of sparkling water
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of melted butter
  • a pinch salt

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs, then add half of the milk and the flour. Mix until combined.
  2. Add the remaining milk, sparkling water, oil, melted butter, and a pinch of salt. Gradually add the rest of the flour, whisking until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  3. Let the batter rest for 20 minutes.
  4. Lightly grease the pan with a few drops of oil before cooking.
    • Repeat this step before each new pancake.
  5. Pour one ladle of batter into the pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Cook for about 1-2 minutes, then flip and cook the other side until lightly browned.
